The first GO-EUREKA workshop (WS1) took place in October 2025 and marked the start of the
project's in-person technical coordination activities.

For workshop material and details, see the WS1 repository:
[IONO-EUREKA/WS1](https://github.com/IONO-EUREKA/WS1).
