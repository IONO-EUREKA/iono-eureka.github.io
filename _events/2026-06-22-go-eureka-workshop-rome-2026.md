The second GO-EUREKA workshop (WS2) is a hybrid event restricted to GO-EUREKA consortium members and invited participants. It aims to review recent scientific advances in ionospheric monitoring of earthquakes and tsunamis, assess progress toward near real-time implementations, and stimulate collaborations within and beyond the consortium.

For workshop material and details, see the WS2 repository:
[IONO-EUREKA/WS2](https://github.com/IONO-EUREKA/WS2).

- **Registration / participation survey**: [evento.renater.fr](https://evento.renater.fr/survey/cassini-day-and-2d-go-eureka-workshop-2esv5al4)
